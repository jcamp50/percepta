### Goals
- Define pricing model
- Calculate unit economics
- Build revenue projections

  - Support costs

- Pricing model document
- Financial projections

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Can't recruit beta customers
**Mitigation**: Offer free access, focus on interviewed streamers, partner with communities

### Risk 2: Low engagement
**Mitigation**: Improve onboarding, add email reminders, create usage guides

### Risk 3: High churn
**Mitigation**: Collect feedback early, iterate quickly, add requested features

### Risk 4: Technical issues
**Mitigation**: Monitor closely, fix bugs immediately, have rollback plan
